Frequently Asked Questions

If you've read the F.A.Q. below, but still have questions, or want to contact us about something else, please send us an email and we'll help you with anything you need.

What is your attraction?

Grim Trails is an award-winning fairy-tale and folklore themed haunted trail with scenes woven throughout small buildings and woods. Don’t believe us? Check out our preview video!

What age groups are appropriate?

While we believe Halloween is for everyone, we do not recommend our haunted trail for children under 10 due to the fright factor. Children under the age of 10 will receive the same terrifying show as any other patron for the same price.

Will the monsters touch me?

Our haunted house has a no-touch policy- our actors and staff will not touch you, and customers are not allowed to touch us. Due to low-light conditions and narrow passages, our monsters may bump into you. Our creatures will not push, grab or maul you.

Does your haunted house have security?

Yes! We value the safety of our staff and guests. We have LMPD officers present at all times when the attraction is open.

Is it different from last year?

During the ten months of the year that we are closed, we endlessly renovate and add new scenes designed to scare and excite. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for sneak peeks of everything we’re dishing up this year.

What form of payment do you accept

We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard and Discover. Paypal as well!

Is it really Scary?

We certainly hope so!

If I get too scared and have to leave through an emergency exit, do I get a refund?

No.

Do you use strobe lights?

Yes. We use strobe lights, fog, snow and laser machines.
